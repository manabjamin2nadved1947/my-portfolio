Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in Combinatorial Optimization, with a focus on designing on-line, streaming, and approximation algorithms.

I am motivated by questions that connect the fields of Computational Geometry and Machine Learning Theory. My current research direction includes sequential decision-making (e.g., multi-armed bandits, online sorting), online geometric data analysis (e.g., streaming clustering, online epsilon nets), and core geometric optimization (e.g., variants of the Traveling Salesman Problem with Neighborhoods (TSPN)).
